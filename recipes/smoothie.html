Smoothie Recipe

The process of making a smoothie.

The following are the instructions and ingredients to make a smoothie. During my childhood I would just add whatever. Though one day things changed and I decided to have instructions in my life. Through this learning process I realized I could replicate my smoothie process daily, so I can always enjoy the smoothie liked the most. This is my story of my smoothie making process.

Ingredients:

  • Banana
  • Blueberries
  • Strawberries
  • Other berried if you want
  • Ice
  • Plain yogurt
  • sugar or honey (whichever you like)
Steps:
  1. Grab blender and insert all the ingredients into blender
  2. Cover blender
  3. Blend
  4. Pour into cup
  5. Drink it and you will enjoy the repetition
