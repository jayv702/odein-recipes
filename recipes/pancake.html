Pankcake Recipe

The process of making a pancake.

The following are the instructions and ingredients used to make pancakes. Pancakes are best eaten in the morning, though some individuals may prefer to eat it whenever. Any time of day you like, I will not judge you for it, for pancakes can be delicious and fullfilling if prepared properly. FIN.

Ingredients:

  • egg
  • flour
  • milk
  • love
  • cooking oil (prerferably butter)
Steps:
  1. Mix egg, flour, and milk in a bowl.
  2. Pour butter in heated pan.
  3. Pour batter into pan.
  4. Cook one side for a bit, checking the bottom with a spatula until light brown.
  5. Flip onto other side and cook until other side light brown.
